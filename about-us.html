About Us

The Caribbean Christmas recipe website, Caribbean Flavours.com, is the go-to destination for those seeking an authentic taste of the Caribbean during the holiday season. The site, run by award-winning cookbook author and chef Chris De La Rosa, offers a plethora of traditional West Indian Christmas recipes, such as crab-stuffed brown fish stew, brown sugar and passion fruit glazed ham, and more.

Visitors to the site can also indulge in the famous Caribbean rum, which is often incorporated into many holiday recipes. The friendly and informative tone of the site makes it easy for anyone to try their hand at these mouth-watering dishes. Whether you're a long-time resident of the Caribbean or a first-time visitor, Caribbean Flavours.com provides an excellent opportunity to experience the authentic flavors of the region during the holidays.
