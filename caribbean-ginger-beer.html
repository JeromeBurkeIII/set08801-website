Caribbean ginger beer

Recipe Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh ginger
  • Lime peel from 1 lime
  • 8 cups of water
  • Granulated sugar (to taste)
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 teaspoon of cloves
  • 1 tablespoon raw rice
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla essence
  • Angostura Bitters

DIRECTIONS

  1. Wash and chop ginger up into chunks
  2. Blend ginger with 3 cups of water
  3. Add lime peel, rice, cinnamon stick to the ginger mixture and leave to set overnight (24-48 hours)
  4. Strain mixture
  5. Add the remaining water to the mixture and sweeten to taste (Add additional water if needed)
  6. Add a dash of Angostura bitters, vanilla essence, and rum if adding
  7. Pour into a clean bottle or jug
  8. Add cloves and refrigerate
  9. Serve on crushed ice or chilled
